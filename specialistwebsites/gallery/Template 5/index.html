Tasmania · Private practice

Steady hands. Clear answers.

Dr Marcus Holloway, FRACS — hip & knee arthroplasty, sports knee injury, and regional outreach clinics on the North West Coast.

Patients from across lutruwita / Tasmania travel to Hobart for complex joint surgery, then recover closer to home with local physiotherapists and their usual GP coordinating medications and wound reviews under shared care models common in the Australian system.

Referrals are triaged from general practitioners statewide and from the Royal Hobart Hospital orthopaedic unit where clinically appropriate. Fees, health fund rebates, and the Medicare Safety Net should be discussed with the practice manager before admission.
