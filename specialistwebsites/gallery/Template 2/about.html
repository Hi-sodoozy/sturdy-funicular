Background

Dr Okonkwo trained in Brisbane and completed advanced training in community paediatrics. He works closely with maternal and child health nurses, schools, and allied health providers across South East Queensland.

He is registered with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA) and participates in continuing professional development through the RACP CPD program.

His philosophy is straightforward: listen to parents and carers, explain options clearly, and align care with National Health and Medical Research Council (NHMRC) endorsed guidance where it exists.
