About Dr Whitmore

Dr Sarah Whitmore completed her medical degree at the University of Melbourne and fellowship with the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners. She has worked in community health across Victoria and is committed to culturally safe care for all patients.

Her clinical interests include cardiovascular risk reduction, diabetes care aligned with Australian guidelines, and supporting patients navigating the Medicare system, including chronic disease management plans and Team Care Arrangements where appropriate.

Outside of clinical work, she contributes to local GP education networks and stays current with Therapeutic Guidelines and RACGP standards.
